The FDA credited a hard clinical endpoint, not a surrogate biomarker move. That distinction reshapes payer conversations entirely, and it raises the bar for any competitor trying to follow with a lipid-panel-only story.

The company built one of the deepest antisense platforms in biopharma, but the knock has always been the same — brilliant science, underwhelming revenue.
