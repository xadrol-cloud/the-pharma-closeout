Boehringer Ingelheim acquired exclusive ex-Korea/China/Hong Kong rights to Hanmi's third-generation EGFR TKI HM61713 for $50M upfront and up to $680M in milestones plus tiered double-digit royalties. Boehringer terminated the deal September 30, 2016 following clinical safety signals (interstitial lung disease deaths).
