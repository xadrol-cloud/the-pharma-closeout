Lilly and Hanmi entered an exclusive worldwide (excluding China, Hong Kong, Korea) license and collaboration for HM71224, an oral BTK inhibitor for autoimmune diseases. Hanmi received $50M upfront with up to $640M in development, regulatory, and sales milestones plus tiered double-digit royalties. Lilly terminated the deal in 2019 after Phase 2 RA trial failed.
