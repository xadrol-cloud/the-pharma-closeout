MacroGenics granted Janssen a global, exclusive license to MGD015, a preclinical CD3 x tumor-target bispecific DART molecule. MacroGenics received $75M upfront plus up to $665M in clinical, regulatory, and commercialization milestones (~$740M total) plus tiered royalties on global sales. Second deal between the parties (followed 2014 MGD011 deal).
