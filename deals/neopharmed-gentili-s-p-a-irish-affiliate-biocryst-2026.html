BioCryst granted an Irish affiliate of Neopharmed Gentili exclusive rights to commercialize navenibart for hereditary angioedema in Europe. BioCryst receives $70M upfront, up to $275M in regulatory and sales milestones, and tiered royalties of 18% to 30% on net sales. Builds on the prior 2025 sale of BioCryst's European ORLADEYO business to Neopharmed Gentili.
