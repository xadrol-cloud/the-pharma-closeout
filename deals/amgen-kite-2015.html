Amgen and Kite Pharma announced a strategic research collaboration and license agreement to develop and commercialize CAR T cell immunotherapies based on Kite's engineered autologous cell therapy platform and Amgen's array of cancer targets. Kite received $60M upfront and was eligible for up to $525M in milestones per Amgen program plus tiered royalties; Amgen also eligible for $525M per Kite program.
