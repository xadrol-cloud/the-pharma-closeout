BMS gained an exclusive option to license PROSTVAC, Bavarian Nordic's Phase 3 PSA-targeting cancer immunotherapy. Bavarian Nordic received $60M upfront, with $80M option exercise plus up to $230M survival-tiered, $110M regulatory, and $495M sales milestones. Phase 3 PROSPECT trial later failed to meet OS endpoint (2017).
