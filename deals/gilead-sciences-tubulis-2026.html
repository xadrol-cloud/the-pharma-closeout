Gilead acquired German ADC specialist Tubulis for $3.15B upfront (cash-free, debt-free) plus up to $1.85B in milestones (~$5B total). The acquisition closed on May 21, 2026. It brings lead asset TUB-040 (NaPi2b TOPO1i ADC in Phase 1b/2 for platinum-resistant ovarian cancer; FDA Fast Track), TUB-030 (5T4 ADC), and a next-gen ADC platform. The Tubulis team remains in Munich as The Tubulis ADC Innovation Center, a dedicated next-gen ADC hub within Gilead.
